Mission

Capital. Legacy.
Compounded.

SCIA Impact Fund is the official social impact investment and philanthropy arm of Southern California Indo-Americans (USC SCIA). We deploy capital into high-potential Indian startups and mission-driven nonprofits in education, healthcare, economic empowerment, and South Asian community development.

Our model is built for generational legacy: the full portfolio and cash reserves are gifted directly to the next e-board, ensuring compounding impact for decades. Every check written is a chapter in a continuum that outlasts any single class.

Approach

Institutional Process.
Generational Conviction.

01
Professional-Grade Process

Genuine venture and private equity framework: deal sourcing, structured diligence, term evaluation, and portfolio monitoring. The same rigor expected of top-tier institutional funds.

02
Dual Mandate Architecture

Equity investments into high-potential startups and strategic philanthropic grants into mission-aligned nonprofits treated as a unified deployment strategy. Not separate programs.

03
Centralized Decision-Making

Capital allocation grounded in rigorous diligence and informational edge. Centralized leadership. Measured conviction. No committee drift, no cultural sentiment.

04
Asymmetric Compounding

We think in decades. The portfolio handed to the next class is the most important return metric we track. Short-cycle noise is ignored. Generational compounding is the mandate.

Team & Governance

Led by Election.
Guided by Experience.

SCIA Impact Fund is run by the elected e-board of Southern California Indo-Americans at USC. The core team is supported by a distinguished Advisory Board of alumni and South Asian leaders in finance, venture capital, and private equity.

Governance is democratic in its foundation and institutional in its execution. Leadership changes. The Fund endures.

Process

Our selection consists of two focused interviews.

Round 01
Behavioral Interview

We explore clarity of thought, intellectual curiosity, and alignment with disciplined, multi-generational capital allocation. No finance background required for this round.

Round 02
Technical & Case Round

Includes investment analysis, brain teasers, real-world deal scenarios, and EQR-style exercises designed to surface analytical edge and the ability to ignore market noise.

Our Commitment

Our process is intentionally straightforward and merit-based. We welcome candidates of all backgrounds and cognitive styles. Neurodivergent applicants or those who process information differently are strongly encouraged to apply. We are happy to adapt the format if a non-traditional approach better allows you to demonstrate your capabilities.

All roles at the Associate or Analyst level are open to current SCIA members. Exceptional candidates may receive meaningful downstream carry participation and hands-on exposure to the Fund's proprietary AI CFO platform.
