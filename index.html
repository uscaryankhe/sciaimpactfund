Our Mandate

Two Lines of
Deployment.

SCIA Impact Fund deploys capital across two strategies simultaneously — equity investments into high-potential Indian startups, and strategic grants into mission-aligned nonprofits. Not separate programs. A unified mandate.

Investments
  • Deal sourcing across the Indian startup ecosystem
  • Structured due diligence, EQR memos & term evaluation
  • Active portfolio monitoring and equity research
  • Informational edge — not market sentiment
Philanthropy
  • Strategic grants to mission-aligned nonprofits
  • Focus: education, healthcare, economic empowerment
  • Rigorous nonprofit diligence and social ROI measurement
  • 100% asset transfer to the next e-board
