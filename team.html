Election & Continuity

The e-board is elected annually by SCIA membership. At the end of each term, the outgoing board transfers the full portfolio — investments, philanthropic positions, and cash reserves — to the incoming class. The Fund's capital compounds. Its mission never resets.

Accountability

Leadership is accountable to the SCIA membership, not to any individual agenda or external pressure. Capital allocation decisions are made by the e-board with Advisory Board counsel — never by committee sentiment or cultural obligation.
