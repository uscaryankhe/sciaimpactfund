Decisions & Offers
Process

Our selection consists of two focused interviews.

Round 01
Behavioral Interview

We explore clarity of thought, intellectual curiosity, and alignment with disciplined, multi-generational capital allocation. No finance background required for this round.

Round 02
Technical & Case Round

Includes investment analysis, brain teasers, real-world deal scenarios, and EQR-style exercises designed to surface analytical edge and the ability to ignore market noise.

Our Commitment

Our process is intentionally straightforward and merit-based. We welcome candidates of all backgrounds and cognitive styles. Neurodivergent applicants or those who process information differently are strongly encouraged to apply. We are happy to adapt the format if a non-traditional approach better allows you to demonstrate your capabilities.

All roles at the Associate or Analyst level are open to current SCIA members. Exceptional candidates may receive meaningful downstream carry participation and hands-on exposure to the Fund's proprietary AI CFO platform.

Interested in joining the team?

Reach out directly. We review applications on a rolling basis and prioritize candidates who demonstrate genuine conviction.
